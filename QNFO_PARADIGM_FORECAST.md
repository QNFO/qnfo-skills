The QNFO 100-Year Paradigm Forecast applies the Cascading Bayesian Foresight Engine (CFPE) to evaluate four-era paradigm shifts spanning quantum computing (2026–2036), bio-mimetic spintronics (2036–2046), hydrodynamic quantum mechanics (2046–2076), and pre-geometric unification (2076–2126+). After rigorous evidence synthesis, multi-adversarial red-team auditing, and Phase 0 MVP verification, the forecast reaches an evidence-adjusted cascade expected value of **~13.5%** (revised from an initial 52.4%).

### Key Findings

1. **Kapitza Thermal Ceiling Confirmed:** Per-qubit heat generation exceeds Kapitza boundary conductance by 4,000× at 50 mK — the thermal wall is a genuine physical constraint, hitting earlier than expected (hundreds of qubits, not thousands).

2. **FCI Contradiction Identified:** The foundational QNFO paper (`reassessing-the-foundations-of-quantum-computation`) refutes the Era 1 FCI pivot by demonstrating that topological QC fails at nonzero temperature due to thermal anyon proliferation. Era 1 must shift to an ultrametric-first approach.

3. **Era 2 Blocked Without Experimental Data:** Zero synthetic hydrogel spin relaxation measurements exist. Biological quantum coherence operates on femtosecond timescales — a 9–12 order of magnitude gap from the millisecond-second coherence needed for computation.

4. **Bell Loophole Open:** The Measurement Independence assumption in Bell's theorem is logically relaxable, providing theoretical possibility for local hydrodynamic Bell-violation reproduction. Empirically indistinguishable from standard QM.

5. **NV-Diamond Currently Preferred:** With ~1 ms T₂ at 300 K and 20+ years of active research vs. zero bio-mimetic data, NV-diamond is the preferred Era 2 pathway pending experimental hydrogel results.

6. **Institutional Inertia Confirmed:** Major QC companies have retreated from "quantum supremacy" to "quantum utility" — an ambition downgrade without paradigm acknowledgment. Adds 5–10 years to Era 1 timeline.
